Account Deletion

Quirky Ball · Developer contact: chkwnsgml3@gmail.com

Fastest way — delete directly in the app

  1. Open the Quirky Ball app.
  2. Go to Settings from the home screen.
  3. Tap Delete Account and follow the confirmation steps.

Once confirmed, all game data stored on our servers and your device is permanently deleted immediately and cannot be recovered.

No access to the app — request by email

Use the button below to email us. Please include your nickname and display code (shown at the bottom of the in-app Settings screen) so we can verify your account. We will delete the account and reply within 7 days.

Request account deletion by email

Data that is deleted or retained

Data typeDeletion / retention policy
Guest identifier (UID), nickname, display code, countryDeleted immediately in-app, or within 7 days of an email request
Linked Google account info (email, profile name)Deleted on the same timeline
Server-side game records (e.g., best score)Deleted on the same timeline
Leaderboard entriesDeleted with the account; they also expire automatically after 7 days even without a request
Recovery codeInvalidated when the account is deleted
Device-only data (virtual currency, language setting)Never stored on our servers; removed when you delete the app
Advertising identifier (ADID)Managed by Google under its own privacy policy (Google policy)

We retain data after deletion only where a longer retention period is required by applicable law; otherwise no data is kept.
